# 🎂 Akari's Anime Rescue: Birthday Quest 🎮

Welcome to **Akari's Anime Rescue: Birthday Quest**, a personalized 2D side-scrolling platformer web game built in React + Vite. This game is designed as a special, interactive birthday present for **Akari**!

---

## 📖 The Story: Multiverse Rescue

On the morning of her birthday, Akari wakes up to find a multiverse anomaly: her favorite anime worlds have collapsed into a mysterious pixelated citadel. Worse, the **Gloom Monsters** have locked her favorite anime companions inside mystical cages, stealing the elements of her birthday celebration!

As Akari, the player must travel through the pixelated realms, defeat the Gloom Monsters, and rescue the heroes:
- **Muichiro Tokito** (Demon Slayer)
- **Chuuya Nakahara** (Bungo Stray Dogs)
- **Giyu Tomioka** (Demon Slayer)
- **Luka Couffaine / Luca** (Miraculous Ladybug)
- **Yuta Okkotsu** (Jujutsu Kaisen)
- **Vanitas** (The Case Study of Vanitas)

Once rescued, each character follows Akari in a classic retro party-train style, granting her unique birthday powers to help her defeat the final boss and unlock a grand, interactive birthday surprise!

---

## 🎮 Game Controls

- **Left / A**: Move Left
- **Right / D**: Move Right
- **Space**: Jump
- **X / Enter**: Attack / Action (to break cages and defeat monsters)
